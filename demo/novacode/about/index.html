About NovaCode

Digital skills for every South African learner

We're a South African education company on a mission: make world-class coding and robotics education available to every learner and every teacher in the country — no matter the school, the province or the budget.

  1. Home
  2. About NovaCode

Our story

NovaCode Academy started with a simple observation: South Africa's new Coding & Robotics curriculum was arriving faster than schools could prepare for it. Learners were eager, parents were asking — but most schools had neither trained teachers nor working equipment.

So we built the missing piece: a partner that brings the whole programme — coaches, curriculum, kits and teacher training — to any school, anywhere in the country. Today our programmes run in all nine provinces, from metro independent schools to rural no-fee schools, in person and live online.

Our mission

To give every South African learner from Grade R to matric a genuine, progressive digital-skills education — and to empower the teachers who will carry it forward. We measure success in two numbers: learners who can build things with technology, and teachers who can teach it without us.

🎯

Learner-first

How we work
