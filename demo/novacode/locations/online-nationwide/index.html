NovaCode in Online — Nationwide

Not near one of our regional teams? Every NovaCode programme has a live online format. Our virtual classrooms are coach-led and interactive — small groups, cameras on, real projects — nothing like passively watching videos.

Online lessons run on a weekly timetable in the afternoons and early evenings, with learners grouped by age and experience level. All a learner needs is a computer and a stable connection; we handle the rest, including all software setup in the first session.

Our remote educator training has reached teachers in all nine provinces, from Limpopo to the Northern Cape — live workshop series with practical assignments between sessions and a national community of practice.
