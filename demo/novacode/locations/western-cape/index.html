NovaCode in Western Cape

The Western Cape leads the country in tech employment per capita, and its schools increasingly expect real digital-skills programmes, not once-off demos. NovaCode Academy partners with schools in Cape Town, the northern suburbs, Stellenbosch, Paarl, and along the Garden Route through George and Knysna.

Our Western Cape programmes lean into the province's strong maker culture: robotics leagues, inter-school hackathons and holiday bootcamps run alongside weekly clubs and timetabled lessons.

For schools in the winelands and Garden Route towns, we combine periodic on-site intensives with live online lessons — so distance from the metro never limits a learner's progress.
