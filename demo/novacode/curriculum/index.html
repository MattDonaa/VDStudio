📚 Curriculum

One pathway, Grade R to matric

Our curriculum is a single, deliberate journey: every year builds on the last, from unplugged logic games in Grade R to portfolio-ready Python and robotics projects in Grade 12 — aligned with the DBE Coding & Robotics curriculum throughout.

  1. Home
  2. Curriculum Gr R–12

🧸 Foundation Phase — Thinking Like a Coder Grade R–3

Computational thinking without pressure: sequencing, patterns, cause-and-effect and simple visual coding.

  • Unplugged coding games and logic play
  • ScratchJr storytelling and simple animations
  • Early robotics with screen-free floor robots
  • Digital citizenship basics, age-appropriately

🎮 Intermediate Phase — The Block Years Grade 4–6

Learners master the grammar of programming in Scratch while building genuinely impressive projects.

  • Loops, conditionals, variables and events in Scratch
  • Game design and interactive animations
  • Intro robotics: sensors, motors and simple builds
  • First hackathon-style team challenges

🐍 Senior Phase — Blocks to Text Grade 7–9

The pivotal transition to real programming languages, aligned with the DBE Coding & Robotics curriculum.

  • Python fundamentals: logic, functions, data
  • HTML, CSS and first web pages
  • Microcontroller robotics and electronics
  • Structured problem-solving and debugging skills

🚀 FET Phase — Portfolio & Beyond Grade 10–12

Learners specialise and build a portfolio that opens doors to university and industry.

  • Advanced Python: data structures, APIs, automation
  • Full web projects with JavaScript and databases
  • Competition robotics and engineering projects
  • Portfolio building, plus IT/CAT subject support
Delivery

Every phase, delivered your way

The full pathway is available through timetabled lessons, after-school clubs, holiday bootcamps and live online classes — and teachers can be trained to deliver it themselves through our educator training.
