🧑‍🏫 Educator Training

Teachers first. Everything else follows.

The fastest way to scale digital skills in South Africa is to empower teachers. Our educator training programmes take teachers from zero to confidently delivering coding and robotics lessons — with SACE-endorsed CPD points, ready-made lesson plans and ongoing support.

Why it works

Training built by teachers, for teachers

Audience: Teachers, HODs and school leaders — no coding background required.

SACE CPD points

Our workshops are structured for continuing professional development recognition.

Zero-to-classroom

Teachers leave with lesson plans, assessments and resources they can use the very next day.

Ongoing mentorship

Training doesn't end at the workshop — we support educators with check-ins and a community of practice.

Aligned to the new curriculum

Built around the DBE Coding & Robotics curriculum for Grade R–9 and beyond.
