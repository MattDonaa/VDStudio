🤝 Our Partners

Growing digital skills is a team sport

No single organisation can close South Africa's digital-skills gap alone. NovaCode works with schools, sponsors, NGOs and education bodies — each partnership designed around one goal: more learners building real skills.

  1. Home
  2. Our Partners
🏫

Partner Schools

Over 350 public and independent schools nationwide host our coding clubs, timetabled lessons and robotics programmes — from single-club starts to whole-school digital-skills strategies.

🏢

Corporate & CSI Sponsors

Corporate social-investment partners fund coding clubs and robotics labs at no-fee schools. Sponsorships are ring-fenced per school, with transparent impact reporting every term.

🤲

NGOs & Foundations

We work alongside education NGOs to reach learners in under-resourced and rural communities, combining their community trust with our curriculum, coaches and kit.

🏛️

Education Bodies

We support district and provincial initiatives around the DBE Coding & Robotics curriculum, including educator development programmes and pilot-school rollouts.

🔧

Technology Partners

Leading educational-robotics and edtech providers supply the hardware and platforms behind our programmes, keeping our kit current and classroom-proven.

🎓

Universities & Competitions

We connect senior learners to university outreach programmes and coach teams for national and international coding and robotics competitions.
